What is A NFT

Non-Fungible Token (NFT). An NFT is a digital asset that represents real-world objects like art, music, in-game items and videos. They are bought and sold online, frequently with cryptocurrency.

Why Pursue NFT and an Understanding?

Like physical money, cryptocurrencies are fungible, meaning that they can be traded or exchanged, one for another.

Become A NFT Artist!

NFT art is a totally new way of categorizing digital artworks that enables designers to monetize their work. It's supposed to be a quicker process and a more accessible way for designers to produce work and reap the rewards for their creativity.
